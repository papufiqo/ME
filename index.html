Who We Are

Built on a decade of evidence

Nexus M&E Consultants was founded in 2015 with a single conviction: that rigorous evidence and honest evaluation are the most powerful tools for making development interventions work.

We are a team of nine seasoned professionals — economists, data scientists, evaluators, and sector specialists — who have spent careers turning complex project data into actionable insights.

Our work spans humanitarian response, climate adaptation, public health, education, governance, and private sector development across four continents. We don't just measure outcomes — we help our clients understand why things happened, and what to do next.

Why Choose Us

What sets us apart

Independent & Credible ›
We operate with complete methodological independence. Our evaluations are never shaped by client preferences — only by evidence. This integrity is the foundation of our reputation.
Sector-Deep Expertise ›
Our nine specialists bring deep sector expertise in health, education, livelihoods, climate adaptation, and governance — ensuring evaluations reflect the nuances of each domain.
Data-First, People-Centered ›
We combine cutting-edge quantitative analytics with deep qualitative understanding of community contexts — producing findings that are both statistically robust and humanly meaningful.
Actionable, Not Academic ›
Our deliverables are designed for decision-makers, not journal editors. Clear recommendations, visual reporting, and facilitated learning sessions ensure findings actually change practice.
Local Rooted, Globally Connected ›
With an active network of 60+ local consultants and research partners across Africa, Asia, and Latin America, we deliver culturally-grounded evaluations at global scale.
